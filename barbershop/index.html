The shop

Trained the old way, before they trained anyone else.

Brass & Blade opened in 1998 above what used to be a hardware store, and never moved. All three barbers came up through apprenticeships, not certificates — which means fades that actually fade, and shaves that don't nick. We don't take walk-ins lightly, and we don't watch the clock while you're in the chair.
