The shop

Run by two people who couldn't find a good cup nearby.

Waypoint opened in 2019 out of a shared frustration: great coffee shouldn't require a fifteen-minute drive. We roast in small batches, change the menu with the seasons, and know most of our regulars by their order before they say a word.
